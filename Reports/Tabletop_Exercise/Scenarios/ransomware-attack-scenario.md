# Scenario: Ransomware Attack Leading to Data Encryption

## Background

A medium-sized company experiences a ransomware attack that encrypts critical data across multiple departments. The ransomware demands a substantial payment in cryptocurrency to decrypt the data.

## Timeline

- **09:00 AM:** IT department detects unusual activity on the network, including unexpected data encryption processes.
- **09:15 AM:** Employees report being unable to access essential files and receive ransom notes on their screens.
- **09:30 AM:** The IT team confirms a ransomware infection affecting key servers and workstations.
- **10:00 AM:** The Incident Commander convenes the incident response team to assess the situation.
- **10:30 AM:** Decisions are made regarding containment, eradication, and communication strategies.

## Affected Areas

- **IT Systems:** Servers, workstations, and network infrastructure.
- **Data:** Sensitive company and client information.
- **Operations:** Business continuity and service delivery.

## Assumptions

- The ransomware has not yet propagated to backup systems.
- Initial access was gained through a phishing email containing a malicious attachment.
- Not all employees have received cybersecurity awareness training.

## Objectives

- Contain the spread of the ransomware.
- Eradicate the threat from all affected systems.
- Restore encrypted data from backups.
- Communicate effectively with internal and external stakeholders.
- Identify and address security gaps to prevent future incidents.
